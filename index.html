GEOMATICS ENGINEERING

Geomatics is the study of land and sea using surveys, mapping and Geographic Information Systerm (GIS) to build highly accurate digital profile of our environment. This is then used to make informed decision about the best place to build roads, bridges, buildings, offshores oil rigs and much more besides. Geomaticians also use satellite, laser and 3D technology to create the maps of tomorrow. In the past, the primary career in geomatics was surveying but today geomatics encompasses photogrammetry, cartography, geographic information system, remote sensing and many more.

Surveying deals with inspection, planning, design and implementation of terrestrial boundaries. It is the art, science and technology of making all essential measurements to determine and establish the relative position of points together with their physical and cultural details.

Professional surveying refers to the application of knowledge of the science of surveying measurement, the legal principles of boundary location, the laws related to boundaries and land use, the applicable mathematical and computational theories and principles, the natural and other forces which affect positional accuracy, the land planning and development concepts pertinent to subdivision of land and property surveys, land record and land tenure concepts, geodetic and other earth related sciences to the analysis, design and execution of surveying and mapping projects and the design of land mapping and information systems. Therefore the surveyor is primarily an analyst.
